How to play game

1. When you select game2, Colorline, from main menu in main.py
2. Game2.py will run automatically
3. In this game, player mush have 2 persons
4. First person choose number of rows(1 to 6) that want to drop your own chip, take turns to each other
5. If chips of one player have ordered by four in both of horizontal and vertical as well as aslant line, get wins
6. When get the winner, it shows result
