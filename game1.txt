How to play game

1. When you select game1, Blackjack, from main menu in main.py
2. Game1.py will run automatically
3. Program shows what card in your hand and also in computer hand are, by hiding another card of computer first
4. There is menu which asks you to choose between hit or stick by:
   - Hit: random another card from pile
   - Sticks: put all of your cards to battle with computer
5. The program will calculate the total value of your cards
6. If your value less than computer or not get close to Blackjack, 21, you will gets lost
7. Conversely, you will be a winner if your value more than computer or close to 21
8. Game finished, turn back to main menu
